# Downgrade Plan

**Plans are upgrade-only. Downgrades are not supported.** Attempting to move to a lower tier returns `downgrade_not_supported`.

## Why

A higher tier holds more atoms than a lower one — for example, Professional stores up to 100,000 atoms and Solo up to 10,000. A larger substrate cannot be fitted onto a smaller one without dropping atoms, and losing your memory is not an acceptable outcome. Rather than silently delete or partially migrate your data, we do not offer downgrades at all.

## What to do if you need a smaller plan

- **Stay on your current tier.** You pay only your monthly price — there is no penalty for having headroom you are not using.
- **Reduce your bill by reducing usage.** Your bill is the flat tier price plus any metered atom overage. Tombstoning unused atoms via `session_checkpoint` lowers overage charges, but it does not change your base tier price.
- **Start fresh on a smaller plan.** If you genuinely want a lower tier, [export your atoms](/docs/subscription/cancel), cancel, and sign up again at the smaller tier, then re-import only what you need. Cancellation deprovisions your instance and takes a snapshot with **no free grace period**, so **export first** — see [Cancel Subscription](/docs/subscription/cancel).

## Upgrades

Upgrades **are** supported and take effect immediately — see [Upgrade Plan](/docs/subscription/upgrade).
